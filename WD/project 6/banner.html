M A R I O

Mario, the cheerful hero of the Mushroom Kingdom, is known for his blue overalls, red cap, and signature moustache. A trusted friend of Princess Peach, he's famous for his bravery alongside his brother Luigi. Skilled in sports like tennis, golf, and kart racing, Mario is a plumber with many talents. Using his expert jumping and power-ups, he battles his archrival, Bowser.
