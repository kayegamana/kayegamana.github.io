BLACKPINK
JISOO | JENNIE | ROSÉ | LISA

BLACKPINK, composed of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa, debuted under YG Entertainment in August 2016 with Square One. They gained global recognition with hits like 2018’s Square Up EP, which reached No. 40 on the Billboard 200, and their debut studio album The Album (2020), peaking at No. 2. Their second album, Born Pink (2022), debuted at No. 1, making them the first K-pop girl group to achieve this feat. With chart-topping tracks like "DDU-DU DDU-DU," BLACKPINK has made history, including being the first Asian act to headline Coachella in 2023, and collaborating with stars like Lady Gaga, Selena Gomez, and Dua Lipa.
